Privacy Policy

Board Displayer Lite

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Board Displayer Lite handles user information.

Information Collection

Board Displayer Lite does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The application operates entirely on the user's device and does not require user accounts, login systems, or personal identifiers.

Local File Access

Board Displayer Lite allows users to open and visualize PCB boardview CAD files selected locally from their device storage.

These files remain fully on the device and are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any external server.

Data Usage

Board Displayer Lite does not use:

• Analytics services
• Advertising networks
• Tracking technologies
• Remote servers for data storage

All processing of boardview data is performed locally on the device.

Third-Party Services

This application does not integrate third-party services that collect user information.

Children's Privacy

Board Displayer Lite does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If the functionality of the application changes in the future, this privacy policy may be updated to reflect those changes.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact the developer.
