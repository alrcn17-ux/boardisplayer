Privacy Policy

BoarDisplayer Lite

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BoarDisplayer Lite handles user information.

Information Collection

BoarDisplayer Lite does not collect, store, process, or transmit any personal data.

The application operates entirely on the user's device and does not require user accounts, login systems, or personal identifiers.

Local File Access

BoarDisplayer Lite allows users to open and visualize PCB boardview CAD files selected locally from the device storage.

These files remain entirely on the user's device and are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with external servers.

Permissions

The application may request permission to access local storage in order to allow users to open boardview files from their device.

This permission is used only for file selection and visualization and is never used to collect or transmit user data.

Data Usage

BoarDisplayer Lite does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Tracking technologies
  • Remote servers for data storage

All boardview data processing occurs locally on the user's device.

Third-Party Services

BoarDisplayer Lite does not integrate third-party services that collect user information.

Children’s Privacy

BoarDisplayer Lite does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If the functionality of the application changes in the future, this Privacy Policy may be updated accordingly.
